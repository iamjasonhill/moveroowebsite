# Moveroo Quote Subdomain Visual Direction

## Purpose

This repo owns the brand intent for `quotes.moveroo.com.au`. Moveroo imports the handoff and maps it into the shared quote, booking, contact, and customer portal layouts.

## Brand Translation

The quote subdomain should feel like the central Moveroo workspace, not a fleet-domain skin. Use the public Moveroo site's deep teal shell, emerald action colour, mint highlights, and calm white form surfaces.

## Runtime Rules

- Use the standard Moveroo subdomain layout and navigation.
- Keep household quote, vehicle quote, booking, contact, and customer portal available.
- Keep the first quote step quickly reachable on mobile.
- Use a dark teal header, footer, hero, and progress frame where it improves balance.
- Keep typing-heavy form panels light and readable.
- Use emerald for primary CTAs, active steps, progress, selected choices, field focus, and validation emphasis.
- Route public support through the shared `/contact` workspace; do not publish direct phone or email channels on the marketing site.
- Do not place route, road, logistics, or brand motifs behind inputs or validation states.

## Legacy Host Note

`removalists.moveroo.com.au` is not the active main-domain quote target. Treat it as a legacy or historical pilot surface until its role is explicitly confirmed.
